Simple Process

Up and Running in 3 Steps

01

Upload Your Material

Drop any PDF, Word doc, or text file into MemoraX. Storage and processing are handled automatically via Cloudinary — no manual setup required.

02

AI Generates Everything

Google Gemini processes your content and instantly creates flashcards, quizzes, summaries, and document chat context — no manual work needed.

03

Study & Track Progress

Review flashcards, take quizzes, chat with your docs, and monitor your mastery through the analytics dashboard with streaks and visual insights.
